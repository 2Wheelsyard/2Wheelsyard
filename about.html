Our history..

Two brothers, born in a small coastal town near Lisbon, sharing a passion for motorcycles and the dream of one day racing the Dakar Rally.

We started small, with the bikes we could afford at the time, exploring Portugal’s sandy trails and winding through its small villages, simply enjoying the ride.

As time went by, we emigrated to another country, and things began to grow — including our motorcycles. After a few good acquisitions, we found ourselves able to buy two brand-new Yamaha Ténéré 700 Rally Editions.

Now we’re living our dream, one step closer each day — traveling the world whenever family time allows.

This year, we reached a new chapter by lining up at the start of our very first rally roadbook — turning a lifelong dream into reality.

And the journey doesn’t stop here — we’re here to add our story to the motorcycle community, to inspire, share, and contribute however we can.
